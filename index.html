About the Workshop

Supported by collaborative research funding from the National Research Council of Canada’s Artificial Intelligence for Logistics Program.

Project Acknowledgement

ML technologies have been widely adopted across mission-critical domains. Alongside the success of these technologies, deployment introduces significant security and privacy threats. Policymakers are developing regulatory frameworks to enforce AI cybersecurity and privacy requirements, making robust evaluation and compliance increasingly critical.

Prototype Framework Toolset

  • A collection of open-source adversarial challenges
  • Case studies
  • Best-practice guidelines for robust ML development and deployment

Project Objectives

  • Develop a taxonomy of ML model/data attacks & defenses
  • Build an Adversarial ML Threat Matrix
  • Devise defenses for OOD/evasion/poisoning/MIA/AIA, etc.
  • Prototype an evaluation framework for robustness
  • Collect & test open-source security tools
  • Curate ground-truth datasets for evaluation
